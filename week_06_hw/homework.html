US Guns Deaths Caused by Police in 2015

In 2015, there were over 1,500 people were shot and killed by police officers in the US. This page visualizes the 2015 police shooting data from the Washington Post from three different angles: 1) Age, 2) Race and 3) Mental Illness.

Age

This histogram chart shows the distribution of different age group of the people who were shot by the police in 2015.

Race

This bar chart shows the race group of people who were shot by the police by race in 2015.

Mental Illness

This ring chart shows whether those people shot by police has mental illness or not.
